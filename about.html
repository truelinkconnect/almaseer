About Us

Trusted global trading company.

Al Maseer Al Dhahabi General Trading LLC

Based in Dubai, United Arab Emirates, specializing in the import, export, and re-export of foodstuff and agricultural commodities. With a strong focus on quality, reliability, and long-term partnerships, we serve customers across the Middle East, Asia, Africa, and other international markets.

Founded with the vision of connecting global producers to international buyers, Al Maseer Al Dhahabi has grown into a dependable name in the trade of grains, pulses, edible oils, and essential food products. Operating from the UAE—one of the world’s most strategic trade hubs—allows us to efficiently source, store, and distribute products across borders with speed and precision.

Who We Are

We are more than a trading company—we are supply chain partners to our clients. Our team brings together deep market knowledge, international sourcing expertise, and a strong logistics network to ensure that every transaction meets global standards.

By working closely with reliable farmers, processors, manufacturers, and exporters, we ensure consistent quality and competitive pricing. Our operations are built on transparency, ethical business practices, and a clear commitment to delivering value in every shipment.

What We Do

At Al Maseer Al Dhahabi General Trading LLC, we manage the complete trade lifecycle—from sourcing at origin to final delivery. Our core activities include:

  • Import & Export of Foodstuff and Agricultural Products
    Supplying high-quality grains, pulses, edible oils, and related food commodities to wholesalers, distributors, retailers, and institutional buyers.
  • Re-Export & International Trading
    Leveraging Dubai’s global connectivity to facilitate smooth re-export operations across multiple regions with optimized logistics and documentation.
  • Bulk & Contract Supply
    Supporting long-term supply agreements and bulk orders for businesses seeking stable, reliable sourcing.

Our structured processes, strong supplier relationships, and efficient logistics enable us to serve both small enterprises and large commercial buyers.
